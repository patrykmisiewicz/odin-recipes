Mint Chicken

A favorite family recipe that my Mom has made for hundreds of guests. Fresh lemon juice and mint leaves are a must! Serve over hot cooked white rice.

Recipe

  • Prep time: 10 mins
  • Cook time: 10 mins
  • Total time: 20 mins
  • Servings: 4
  • Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 42 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • half of a cup all-purpose flour
  • half of a cup margarine
  • quarter cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 quart vegetable oil for frying
  • half of a cup chopped fresh mint leaves

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, mix together the chicken pieces and garlic. Toss with the flour just to coat, and shake off excess.

  2. Heat the margarine in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

  3. Add the chicken pieces; cook and stir until browned.

  4. Add the mint leaves and lemon juice, cover and steam for about 5 minutes, just until the chicken is cooked through.

  5. Do not over cook the chicken or it will be tough.
